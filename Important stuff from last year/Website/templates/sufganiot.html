Hanukkah Sufganiyot

Servings Prep Time Difficulty
15 approx 4 hours Hard

Ingredients

  • 400 g warm rice milk 1 minute in microwave
  • 75 g white sugar dissolve in milk
  • 3½ tsp granulated yeast add to milk and let stand for 10 minutes
  • 45 g margarine melt – 40 seconds in micro at 50% power
  • 1½ tsp vanilla essence add to margarine
  • 60 g honey add to margarine
  • 750 g high-grade flour mix all dry ingredients in a big bowl
  • 3 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Raspberry jam
  • Powdered sugar

Directions

  1. Heat rice milk (1 minute in microwave), dissolve sugar, add yeast and let stand for 10 minutes.
  2. Melt margarine (40 seconds in microwave at 50% power), add honey and vanilla
  3. Mix flour, cornstarch, salt in the main bowl
  4. To the main bowl add milk mixture and margarine mixture.
  5. Mix and knead for 10 minutes. Add rice milk or flour as needed to get a smooth, non-sticky dough.
  6. Let rise for at least 2 hours (more is OK) in an oiled bowl covered with a plastic bag, in a warm 40°C oven (if it is hot outside then you can leave it out, but still cover it)
  7. Divide into equal parts (50gr medium, 70gr large – the recipe makes 1.3kg), make balls with a smooth top, and put on individually cut baking paper, and cover with a towel.
  8. Let rise for 30-60 minutes (no more)
  9. Deep fry in oil 180deg. Lift with the baking paper, and gently lower into hot oil to avoid squishing the Sufgania. Turn over when one side golden brown
  10. Inject jam into each Sufganiya (lightly heat jam to make it easier to inject)
  11. Sift on powdered sugar
