Dumplings

Servings Prep Time Difficulty
30 Dumplings 90 Minutes Medium

Ingredients

    For the dumplings
  • 30 Dumpling Sheets
  • 300g Shredded Cabbage
  • 2 stalks spring onion
  • 200g soft tofu
  • 4-5 finely cut or crushed garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp sesame oil
    • For the sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp sweet chili sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil

Directions

  1. Cut the cabbage, onion, tofu and garlic, and mix them all together.
  2. Add the salt and sesame oil and mix well.
  3. Put the filling in the dumpling sheets
  4. Fill the dumpling sheet on one side with a 1cm border on the side
  5. Close the dumplings by pinching the sheet shut so it makes a half circle
  6. Steam the dumplings for 20 - 25 minutes
  7. Wait for it to cool and serve with the sauce.
