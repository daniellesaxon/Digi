Chocolate Cupcakes

Servings Prep Time Difficulty
15 cupcakes 60 Minutes Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup flour
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 25g cocoa powder
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • ½ Cup oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla essense
  • ¼ tsp salt

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C
  2. Add the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda into a bowl and mix together
  3. Add the water, oil and vanilla and mix
  4. Add into a 9 inch pan or into a cupcake tray
  5. Bake for 20 - 30 minutes
  6. Let cool and serve
