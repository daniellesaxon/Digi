Vanilla Cake

Servings Prep Time Difficulty
8 Slices 60 Minutes Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup soy milk
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • ½ cup oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 ½ cups plain flour
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C and line 8 inch round cake pan
  2. Add the milk and vinegar into a large bowl and let curdle for a few minutes.
  3. Then add oil and vanilla extract, mix and then whisk in the sugar to dissolve
  4. Sift the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt into the bowl and mix until no lumps remain
  5. Bake in the oven for 30 – 40 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
