What is aerobic gymnastics?

Aerobic gymnastics is a sport in which competitors will perform a minute and a half routine to music displaying skills and choreography. This can be in individuals, pairs, trios or groups. It is judges out of 3 categories; artistic, execution and difficultly. These three scores from each category are added up to determine the final score.



How do I use this website?

This website can be used by judges coaches and athletes to help competitions run smoothly. Coaches will enter their athletes skills and level into the competitor page. This information will be passed onto the judges who can easily judge all three categories. Once the final score has been determined, athletes will be able to see their score on the scores page.
